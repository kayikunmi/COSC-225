Self Similar Image

The self-similar image is created using SVG The drawCircleWithDiamond function draws circles and diamonds (two triangles) recursively. Starting with a circle at the center, it draws four diamonds at the corners of the circle, and then recursively repeats this process with a reduced radius and depth for each corner, creating a self-similar pattern. The scale factor determines the size of the circles and diamonds, and the depth controls the level of recursion and complexity of the image.
